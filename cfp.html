Paper and Invited Session Submission

The authors are invited to submit contributions to the ACD2017. Two types of papers are welcomed

  • regular papers - reporting novel contribution to the field of ACD interest,
  • work in progress papers - presenting recent ideas, open problems, and on-going work.

All submitted papers will be peer-reviewed (at least two independent reviewers) and all the accepted and presented papers will be published in IEEE online proceeding. Selected papers will be published in extenso in "Studies In Informatics and Control" journal

Paper Format

All submitted papers (initial submission and final) are strictly limited to 6 pages (IEEE double-column format). The format is available for both LaTeX and MS Word at the IEEE website: https://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html.

Paper Copyright

By submitting a paper, all authors are assumed to accept the terms of the IEEE Publication License published at their website: https://www.ieee.org/publications_standards/publications/rights/copyrightpolicy.html.

Paper Submission

The submission site opens on March 1, 2017 and closes on September 15, 2017. To submit a paper an EasyChair system account is needed. Papers in the PDF format are submitted through the EasyChair conference management system via the submission site: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=acd2017

Invited Session Proposal Submission

Invited session proposals are welcomed. Each proposal should include following information:

  • Title of the proposed session,
  • Names and affiliation of organizer(s) including short bio,
  • Session abstract highlighting its relevance to the ACD2017,
  • List of 5 invited papers including title, authors, and short abstract for each paper.

The proposal should be submitted via e-mail to the IPC chair: ipc.chair@acd2017.eu .
